Services

What we build.

We work on residential and commercial projects across Calgary, Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, and the surrounding counties. If your project doesn't fit neatly into one of these categories, give us a call — we may still be the right fit.

Custom Homes

Architect-led custom builds across Calgary and the foothills, from infill to acreage.

From inner-city infills in Bridgeland and Marda Loop to acreage builds west of the city, we work with your architect — or refer one — to deliver a home built to last in Alberta's climate. ICF foundations, well-insulated envelopes, and finishes that hold up to Calgary's freeze-thaw cycles.

  • Architect collaboration and design-build options
  • ICF and engineered foundations for Alberta soils
  • High-performance envelopes (R-24+ walls, R-60 attics)
  • Triple-pane windows rated for Chinook winds
  • Net-zero-ready and solar-prewired packages

Renovations

Whole-home and major renovations that respect the bones of older Calgary houses.

Many of Calgary's best neighbourhoods — Mount Royal, Hillhurst, Inglewood, Bowness — have homes worth saving. We specialize in respectful, structural-savvy renovations that update plumbing, wiring, and insulation while keeping the character that made you fall in love with the house.

  • Kitchen and bathroom remodels
  • Whole-home renovations and reconfigurations
  • Heritage-sensitive work in established neighbourhoods
  • Knockdown-rebuild evaluations
  • Permits, drawings, and trades fully managed

Commercial & Hospitality

Hotels, resorts, and commercial builds — larger-scale construction with the same hands-on accountability.

Before starting HarbourView, our founder spent years building and renovating hotels, resorts, and commercial buildings, and that work is core to what we do. We take on ground-up commercial construction, hospitality builds and renovations, tenant improvements, and multi-unit residential — phased around occupied sites and held to commercial schedules and inspections.

  • Hotel and resort construction and renovation
  • Ground-up commercial buildings
  • Tenant improvements and commercial fit-outs
  • Multi-unit and mixed-use residential
  • Occupied-site phasing and accelerated schedules

Additions

Second storeys, rear additions, and basement developments that grow with your family.

Outgrowing your home but not your neighbourhood? We add up, out, and down — engineered second storeys, rear extensions, and full basement developments with legal secondary suites where zoning allows. Every addition is engineered for snow load and integrated with your existing roof, framing, and mechanicals.

  • Second-storey additions (engineered)
  • Rear and side additions
  • Basement developments and legal suites
  • Garage builds and garage suites
  • Sunrooms and three-season rooms

Exteriors & Decks

Hardie siding, cedar decks, and outdoor living spaces engineered for Alberta weather.

A Calgary deck has to handle 30°C summer days, –30°C winter nights, and everything Chinook season throws at it. We build with materials and details that survive — proper flashing, hot-dipped fasteners, frost-protected footings, and finishes rated for UV at altitude.

  • Hardie and engineered-wood siding
  • Cedar, composite, and aluminum decking
  • Pergolas, screened porches, and outdoor kitchens
  • Frost-protected footings and helical piers
  • Re-roofing with snow and ice management

Consultation & Design Review

Hourly consultation for buyers, owners, and architects who want a builder's eye early.

Before you buy a fixer-upper, before drawings go to permit, before you sign with another contractor — bring us in. We do paid hourly consultations on feasibility, scope, budgeting, and constructibility. Often it pays for itself ten times over.

  • Pre-purchase walkthroughs
  • Architectural plan review and value engineering
  • Renovation scoping and budget ranges
  • Permit-readiness review
  • Second-opinion estimates
