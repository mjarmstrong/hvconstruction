About

Maritime roots,
Calgary homes.

HarbourView Construction opened its doors in 2026. We're a Calgary-based builder founded by Nova Scotia natives who came west, fell in love with the Bow Valley, and decided it was finally time to put their own name on the work. We bring East Coast craftsmanship — and the directness that comes with it — to every home we build or renovate in Alberta.

Our story

Why a Maritime name on a Calgary builder

Our owners grew up in fishing towns on the South Shore of Nova Scotia, where every house had to stand up to salt air, hurricane gusts, and winters that didn't quit. You learned early that the way a house was framed mattered, that flashing details mattered, that the trades who showed up for your grandfather were the trades you wanted on your own house.

We came to Calgary more than a decade ago, started out swinging hammers for other builders, and quickly realized something: Alberta has its own version of weather punishment — Chinooks, deep frost, dry summers at altitude — and it rewards exactly the same care. After years of running other people's job sites, we incorporated HarbourView in 2026 to do the work the way we always thought it should be done.

The company is brand new, but the hands aren't. Between the two owners we've got more than twenty years on Calgary residential sites — custom homes, infills, and major renovations led personally before we ever hung up our own shingle. The name's a nod to where we're from. The work is firmly here.

How we work

Three things we won't compromise on

Owner on every project

One of the owners walks every site, every week. You'll have their cell number from day one. No layers between you and the person whose name is on the door.

Trades we know

We work with the same electricians, framers, plumbers, and finishers project after project. They know our standards. We know their work. You inherit that relationship.

Honest budgets

We'd rather lose a job to a lower bid than pad ours and surprise you halfway through. Our quotes are line-itemed, our allowances are realistic, and our change orders are signed before work starts.
