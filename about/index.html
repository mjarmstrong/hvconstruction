About

Maritime roots,
Calgary homes.

HarbourView Construction opened its doors in 2026. We're a Calgary-based builder founded by Nova Scotia natives who came west, fell in love with the Bow Valley, and decided it was finally time to put their own name on the work. We bring East Coast craftsmanship — and the directness that comes with it — to every home we build or renovate in Alberta.

Our story

Why a Maritime name on a Calgary builder

Our owners grew up on the Strait of Canso — Mulgrave and Pirate Harbour, where the mainland of Nova Scotia hands off to Cape Breton. It's a working waterfront: salt air, hard winters, and weather that finds every shortcut a builder ever took. You learn early that the way a house is framed matters, that flashing details matter, that the trades who did right by your grandfather are the trades you want on your own house.

We came to Calgary more than a decade ago and started where a lot of good builders start — below grade, doing cribbing and concrete: foundations, walls, and flatwork, the part of the house nobody sees but everything else stands on. From there we moved up into framing, and then into full-scale construction — custom homes, infills, and major renovations. Alberta has its own version of weather punishment — Chinooks, deep frost, dry summers at altitude — and it rewards the exact same care the Strait taught us.

The company is brand new, but the hands aren't. Between the two owners we've got more than twenty years on Calgary residential sites, led personally before we ever hung up our own shingle. We incorporated HarbourView in 2026 to do the work the way we always thought it should be done. The name's a nod to the harbour we're from. The work is firmly here.

How we work

Three things we won't compromise on

Owner on every project

One of the owners walks every site, every week. You'll have their cell number from day one. No layers between you and the person whose name is on the door.

Trades we know

We work with the same electricians, framers, plumbers, and finishers project after project. They know our standards. We know their work. You inherit that relationship.

Honest budgets

We'd rather lose a job to a lower bid than pad ours and surprise you halfway through. Our quotes are line-itemed, our allowances are realistic, and our change orders are signed before work starts.
